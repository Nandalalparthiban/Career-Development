ProtoSem




ProtoSem is Forge’s flagship semester-long program designed for final-year engineering students to transform ideas into working prototypes. It integrates industry exposure, hands-on product development, and entrepreneurship training. Students work in teams, receive expert mentoring, and use advanced labs. The program emphasizes solving societal and industrial challenges. It bridges the gap between academia and industry. ProtoSem equips students with the mindset and skills for innovation-driven careers.
